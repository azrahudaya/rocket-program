ROCKET Program:

Accelerating Businesses for SMEs and Startups

ROCKET Program is a 6-month business acceleration program designed to help small and medium enterprises (SMEs) and startups grow, scale their operations, achieve their goals, and become more competitive in the market.

  • 03

Program Duration: 6 Months

01

Foundation (2 Months)

Training on business fundamentals, including market research, financial planning, and operations management.

02

Growth (2 Months)

Focus on developing business strategies, building teams, and securing funding.

03

Scaling (2 Months)

Learn how to scale businesses, expand reach, and manage growth.

Objectives

  • Enhance Business Skills

    Equip participants with the skills and knowledge needed to run successful businesses.

  • Increase Market Reach

    Help businesses expand their market reach and attract new customers.

  • Secure Funding

    Assist participants in securing funding to grow their businesses.

  • Achieve Sustainability

    Ensure long-term sustainability and success for the participants' businesses.

Program Overview

Mentorship

Networking

Opportunities to network with other participants, mentors, and industry experts.
